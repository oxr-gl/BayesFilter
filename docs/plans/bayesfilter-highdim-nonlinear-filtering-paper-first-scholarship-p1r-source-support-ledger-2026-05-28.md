## Purpose

This ledger records which primary sources can support which parts of the
paper-first rewrite.  It is not a literature survey and not a validation of any
method.  `LOCAL_FULL_TEXT_INDEXED` means the local paper was converted to text
and technical anchors were inspected enough to support a future derivation pass;
it does not mean Codex has verified every proof.
