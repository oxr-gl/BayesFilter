## Purpose

This ledger records P1S source closure for the blocker risks promoted by P1R.
`CLOSED` means the source has enough checked technical anchors for a later
chapter rewrite claim within the listed scope.  It does not mean the proof is
verified, the method works for BayesFilter, or the literature survey is final.

Downloaded PDFs/HTML/JSON under `.local_sources/highdim_nonlinear_filtering/`
are uncommitted cache artifacts.  They are provenance and technical-reading
inputs only; metadata snapshots never support theorem-level claims.
